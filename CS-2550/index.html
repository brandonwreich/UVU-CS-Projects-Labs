Personal Intrests

I am an avid camper. I will go anywhere, anytime to camp, even in the winter. Everything I do is either camping related, or so that I can go camping in the future. Last year I created an LLC called The Mauka Project in order to pay for these expeditions. The Mauka Project is an outdoor expedition company that will plan your outdoor trips as well as provide equipment, guides, and all the fun nature can provide. It definitely helps me pay those camping bills and helps me to go on more trips at other peoples expense. There are so many things you can do on these expeditions. You can go rock climbing, white water rafting, canyoneering, or even have a nice relaxing weekend in the woods. I highly recommend that anyone who is able to should spend as much time as possible in nature.

Another interest I have is music. Music has always been a part of my life. Growing up each sibling and I were required to take piano lessons. Since then I have learned to play several instruments and have even composed a a few pieces of music myself. Unfortunately I don't have as much time to play or learn new instruments, I enjoy listening to music almost 24/7. I will have to say that my favorite genre of music is definitely classical which is controversial to some other peoples views.

What's a computer science major without his geek side. Another interest I have is Star Wars. Anything and everything Star Wars is absolutely amazing and inspired. I constantly watch the movies and tv shows or play the games. Star Wars is one thing that I will never get sick of.
